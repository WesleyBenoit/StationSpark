StationSpark

A safety-first social discovery demo for EV drivers at charging stations. Try the mobile frame to check in, send consent-based invites, open chat, and test Adult Mode gating.

Station-level presence Drivers are shown at charging stations, never at exact GPS coordinates or parking spots.
Invite-before-chat Chat stays locked until a nearby driver accepts a time-limited invitation.
Private Adult Mode Adult preferences appear only after separate opt-in and mutual eligibility.
